[Heading2] About Us
We are one of the LDI license holder companies in Pakistan
HG Telecommunication (Pvt) Ltd was founded in 2022 and obtained its licence in 2023 to provide LDI operation in Pakistan. HG has a licence for the next 20 years and holds a competitive advantage over other LDIs whose licences will be expiring by 2024.
HG Telecommunication&apos;s management has deep roots in the telecom industry. They have experience running LDI business under the name ADG LDI and have established an international voice wholesale aggregator, Link Technology. This gives them an advantage as they already have a deep understanding of the telecommunication business and have already established interconnects with major telecom service providers worldwide.
HG plans to deploy fibre throughout Pakistan to digitise the country and provide services to businesses operating around the country.
