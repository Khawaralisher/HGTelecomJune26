Deep roots in Pakistan’s telecom industry

HG Telecommunication (Pvt) Ltd was founded in 2022 and obtained its licence in 2023 to provide LDI operations in Pakistan. HG holds a licence for the next 20 years and a competitive advantage over other LDIs whose licences are expiring.

HG’s management has deep roots in the telecom industry — with experience running LDI business under the name ADG LDI and establishing the international voice wholesale aggregator Link Technology. This gives us a deep understanding of the business and established interconnects with major telecom providers worldwide.

HG plans to deploy fibre throughout Pakistan — to digitise the country and provide services to businesses operating across the nation.
